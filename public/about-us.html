Learn About Us

At Meldon Energy Solutions Limited, our passion for innovation drives us to deliver reliable and efficient solar lighting systems. With years of expertise in manufacturing and installation, we combine advanced technology with superior craftsmanship to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our commitment to excellence ensures every project is completed to the highest standards, providing long-lasting value and environmental benefits.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize outdoor lighting, Meldon Energy Solutions Limited has grown from a small team of energy enthusiasts to a trusted industry leader. Our journey is marked by continuous growth, strategic partnerships, and a relentless pursuit of quality. We believe in empowering communities through sustainable energy, and our solar street lights have transformed streets, parks, and public spaces nationwide.
