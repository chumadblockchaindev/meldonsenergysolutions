Our Services

Meldon Energy Solutions Limited specializes in the manufacturing and installation of high-quality Solar Street Lights, designed to illuminate communities sustainably. Our expert team delivers end-to-end solutions, from custom design and production to seamless installation and ongoing support. We are committed to providing reliable, energy-efficient lighting that enhances safety, reduces costs, and supports a greener future for urban and rural environments alike.

LW-VE Series Solar Street Light

The LW-VE Series Solar Street Light is engineered for superior outdoor illumination, combining advanced solar technology with robust design. Its high-efficiency solar panels and long-lasting LED bulbs ensure reliable lighting even in challenging environments. Ideal for streets, parking lots, and public spaces, this product offers energy savings, easy installation, and minimal maintenance, making it a sustainable choice for modern urban infrastructure.

LED Street Light

Our LED Street Light delivers powerful, uniform lighting for roads and pathways. Designed for maximum durability and efficiency, it features weather-resistant housing and premium LEDs that provide bright, clear illumination. This product is perfect for municipalities and private developments seeking cost-effective, eco-friendly lighting solutions that enhance safety and visibility.

Main Banner Solar Street Light

The Main Banner Solar Street Light stands out for its sleek design and high-performance solar modules. It is tailored for large-scale installations, offering consistent lighting throughout the night. With intelligent energy management and automatic dusk-to-dawn operation, this street light is an excellent choice for highways, campuses, and commercial areas seeking reliable, green lighting solutions.

Pic3 Solar Street Light

The Pic3 Solar Street Light is designed for versatility and efficiency, providing dependable lighting for residential streets, parks, and walkways. Its integrated solar panel and battery system ensure continuous operation, while the modern design complements any outdoor setting. Choose Pic3 for a blend of style, sustainability, and performance in your lighting projects.
